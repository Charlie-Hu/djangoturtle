Project background

sAdhering to medication treatment is a crucial component for managing chronic conditions, acute illnesses, and overall long-term health and well-being. For patients, caregivers, clinicians and family members, maintaining correct dosages and schedules may be a stressful and dangerous day-to-day challenge. With around 35% of Australians taking prescription medicine every day, we are interested in how an IoT-based medication dispenser / reminder device may help individuals adhere to medica- tion treatments.
