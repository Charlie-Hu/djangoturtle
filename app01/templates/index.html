Project Background

IoT-Based Medicine Reminder and Dispenser developed for Internet of things: Group Project Semester 2, 2022 at University of Western Australia. Team Members:
Nishan Devkar (23762917)
Charlie Hu (23217014)
Ame Liu (22910358)
Alexander Turner (23423442)
Feifan Wu (22951266)

For patients, caregivers, clinicians and family members, maintaining correct dosages and schedules may be a stressful and dangerous day-to-day challenge. Our project aims to help individuals adhere to medication treatments by eliminating the problem of forgetting timely medication intake which could lead to health risks that could have been easily avoided.

User Guide

  1. Turn on the dispenser
  2. Load the Dispenser with pills that are to be dispensed
  3. Go to the webpage: Medicine Reminder@team turtle
  4. If visiting for the first time, click on Register
    1. Enter your Username, Password, email
    2. Click on Register and wait for a few seconds
  5. Once Registered, Login to your account using the login details: username and password
  6. Click on Create Your Plan to create a new reminder schedule
  7. Enter the Medication Details, Number of Pills to dispense, Number of reminders per day and an Emergency Contact email for the patients safety
  8. Click on Save to set the reminder.
  9. Stay healthy,Take care and remember to take your pills!
